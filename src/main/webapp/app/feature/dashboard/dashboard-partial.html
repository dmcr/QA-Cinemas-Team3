About Us

Our cinema

Considered the premier provider of cinema services in the UK, QA Cinemas is committed to quality, experience and world class customer service.


Our site also includes our very own cocktail lounge where the setting is quirky but classy. Most of our creative cocktails are sought after. With a total of 1500 seats, which at the very least are “living room” comfort, QA Cinemas innovative cinema has certainly stamped its footprint on the scene in the UK.


QA Cinema is a new development, hoping to be more than just the 'new kid on the block' - with an all new outlook on the blockbuster experience.
